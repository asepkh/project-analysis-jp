From component parts that play a part in the reliable performance of fuel tanks and sunroofs, to interior and exterior parts that have a high quality feel that harmonizes with the body and cockpit.
Yachiyo engages in integrated production of plastic products, making use of the coating technology we have cultivated since our founding.

Product Line-Up

Interior/Exterior Parts

We provide integrated production of large plastic parts such as bumpers and instrument panels, from molding to painting to packaging. In order to deliver products with a high quality feel that harmonizes with the body and cockpit, we provide products such as outboard engine covers with even higher weather resistance by our extensive molding know-how and painting technology.

Customized Parts

We deliver a variety of customized parts that incorporate Yachiyo's technology to car owners who are particular about their cars.
The exterior parts are designed with a high level of visual quality based on a thorough knowledge of the original body colors, while the interior parts are painted with a special paint that provides a rich color tone and a soft touch, further enhancing the car's appeal.

Looking Ahead to the Evolution of Cars

The evolution of cars is said to include the practical application of electrification and self-driving technology.
The overall weight of a car continues to increase as batteries and motors are installed, as well as autonomous driving technology.
Yachiyo will leverage its knowledge and know-how in the development of plastics and functional parts that it has cultivated over the years to strengthen its technological development of plastic module products that contribute to weight reduction of cars.
