Dividend policy

Yachiyo positions the return of profits to shareholders as one of its most important management issues, and strives to develop its business from a global perspective and improve corporate value.
Regarding the allocation of results, our policy is to comprehensively consider future capital needs, etc., and to pay dividends while taking into account consolidated business results from a long-term perspective.
